1. Who We Are

ZxAI ("ZxAI," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at https://zxai.ai (the "Site"). ZxAI provides AI-powered automation agents for private medical practices in the United States.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information in connection with your use of this Site. It applies to visitors and prospective customers. It does not apply to information handled through ZxAI's service deployments, which run inside the customer's own infrastructure and are governed by the customer's own data policies and applicable Master Service Agreement.

For questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@zxai.ai.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us by email. When you email hello@zxai.ai, we receive your email address, name (if provided), and the content of your message.
  • Request a consultation or demo. If you book a call through a scheduling service linked from our Site, the scheduling provider may collect your name, email address, phone number, and calendar availability. Please review the privacy policy of any third-party scheduling tool you use.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, certain information may be collected automatically by our web hosting infrastructure or by third-party services embedded in the Site:

  • Server and access logs. Standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This is a standard feature of web hosting and is used for security and operational purposes.
  • Google Fonts. Our Site loads typefaces from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font file, your IP address is transmitted to Google's servers. Google's privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. We do not control this data transmission. If you prefer, you can block Google Fonts requests using a browser extension or privacy-focused DNS.
  • Google Analytics 4. We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our Site. GA4 collects information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, referring sources, browser type, device type, and approximate geographic location (derived from IP address). This data is associated with a randomly assigned identifier — not your name or email — and is used solely to improve our Site and marketing. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Information Collected Through the Katie Front Desk Demo

Our Site includes an interactive AI voice and text agent called Katie. When you use the Katie demo, we collect:

  • Session identifier. A randomly generated ID (stored as a cookie named zxai_sid) that persists for 7 days. This allows us to recognize repeat visitors and understand whether a session resulted in a booking.
  • Booking information. If you book a consultation through Katie, we collect your first name, last name, company or practice name, job title, email address, and phone number (optional). This information is stored in our secure database and used to schedule your appointment.
  • Session metadata. We log a non-reversible hash of your IP address (not the IP address itself), your browser's user agent string, the page you were on, and the number of times you have started a Katie session. We do not log audio recordings or the text of your conversation after the session ends.
  • AI processing. Voice and text inputs during a Katie session are processed in real time by Google's Gemini AI API. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Gemini API Terms and its privacy policies. ZxAI does not store audio data after your session ends.

Advertising Cookies (With Consent)

With your consent, we use advertising tags from Google (Google Ads) and Meta (Meta Pixel) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and to show relevant ads to people who have previously visited our Site. These tags are loaded only after you accept advertising cookies through our cookie consent banner. If you decline, these tags are not loaded and no advertising data is collected about your visit.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not use session recording tools. We do not collect payment information through this Site. We do not record or permanently store the audio or text content of Katie conversations.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use a cookie consent banner to give you control over non-essential cookies. The categories below explain what each type of cookie does and when it is set.

Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are required for the Site to function. They cannot be disabled.

  • zxai_sid. A randomly generated session identifier set when you interact with the Katie front desk demo. Used to track whether a session results in a booking and to recognize returning visitors. Expires after 7 days.
  • zxai_consent. Stores your cookie consent preference (accepted or declined). Expires after 365 days.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile. Our bot protection service (Cloudflare Turnstile) may set short-lived cookies to verify that you are a human visitor before connecting you to the Katie demo. These are purely functional and do not track you across sites.

Analytics Cookies (With Consent)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our Site so we can improve it.

  • Google Analytics 4. Sets cookies (_ga, _gid, and related) to count unique visitors, track page views, and measure session behavior. Data is associated with an anonymous identifier, not your name or email. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Advertising Cookies (With Consent)

These cookies are used to measure the performance of our advertising campaigns and to show our ads to people who have previously visited zxai.ai. They are loaded only after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you click "Decline," none of these cookies are set.

  • Google Ads. The Google Ads tag records when a visit originated from one of our ads and enables remarketing to prior visitors. Governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Pixel. The Meta Pixel records site visits and enables remarketing on Facebook and Instagram. Governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.

Managing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie Preferences" in the footer of any page. You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Note that withdrawing consent does not affect any processing that occurred before withdrawal.

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends a GPC signal, we treat it as a request to opt out of advertising cookies and will not load advertising tags for your visit.

9. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), grants you additional rights with respect to your personal information.

Categories of Personal Information Collected

In the past 12 months, we may have collected the following categories of personal information from Site visitors and prospective customers:

  • Identifiers: name, email address, hashed IP address, session identifier (cookie)
  • Professional information: company name, job title (provided during Katie demo booking)
  • Commercial information: records of correspondence and consultation bookings
  • Internet or other electronic network activity: pages visited, browser type, session count, device type
  • Inferences: whether a visitor is likely a prospective customer (derived from session and booking behavior)

Sale and Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information. With your consent, we share certain internet activity data (pages visited, prior visit signals) with Google and Meta for the purpose of showing our own advertisements to prior visitors. Under CPRA, this may constitute "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes. You have the right to opt out of this sharing at any time.

To opt out: Click "Decline" on our cookie banner, or click "Cookie Preferences" in the footer and disable Advertising cookies. We also honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal automatically.

Your Rights

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we have collected, used, disclosed, or shared
  • Delete personal information we have collected about you (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of the sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information (we do not collect sensitive personal information as defined by CPRA)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights

How to Submit a Request

Email hello@zxai.ai with the subject line "California Privacy Request" and describe your request. We will acknowledge receipt within 10 business days and respond within 45 calendar days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

Authorized Agent

You may designate an authorized agent to submit a request on your behalf. We will require written proof of the agent's authorization and may verify your identity directly.
