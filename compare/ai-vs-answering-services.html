Questions we get asked

The honest Q&A

What's the difference between AI staff and a medical answering service?
An answering service picks up the phone and takes a message. AI staff pick up the phone and complete the task. Ruby or PATLive will write down that a patient wants to book, then your team calls back the next morning. ZxAI books the appointment directly in your EHR while the caller is still on the line.
Can an answering service book appointments directly into my EHR?
No. Traditional medical answering services are disconnected from clinical systems. They can only take a message for your team to action later. ZxAI has native EHR integration and books, reschedules, and cancels appointments in real time during the call.
Do answering services handle prior authorization?
No. No traditional answering service handles prior auth. ZxAI's Prior Auth AI staff prepare and submit prior authorization requests, follow up with payers, and alert your team when approvals come in. That saves the average physician 4.6 hours per week.
Is Ruby or PATLive HIPAA compliant?
Most major medical answering services are HIPAA compliant and will sign a Business Associate Agreement. Verify the BAA before using any service. ZxAI does not require a BAA because patient data never leaves your environment. The AI runs inside your infrastructure.
Why pay for AI staff instead of just an answering service?
Same monthly cost, different outcome. An answering service creates a callback queue. AI staff resolve the work without creating one. Every message the answering service takes is still a task your team has to handle. AI staff finish the task.
Can I use both an answering service and AI staff?
Yes, but most practices don't need to. AI staff cover everything an answering service covers plus direct scheduling, EHR integration, prior auth, reactivation, and billing follow-up. Running both is paying for the same coverage twice.
