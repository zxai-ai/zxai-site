Direct scheduling
Answering services take a message. Someone on your team still has to call back, look up availability, and book the appointment — usually the next business day. ZxAI books the slot in real time during the call.

No answering service touches prior auth. ZxAI's Prior Auth Agent submits requests, follows up with payers, and alerts your team when approvals come in. That's 4.6 physician hours per week recovered.
