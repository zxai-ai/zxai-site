About

AI staff that work inside your practice.

ZxAI builds AI staff members for independent medical practices and med spas. Real job functions, running inside your own systems, under your own credentials. No new software. HIPAA-ready architecture.

What we do

Staff, not software.

Independent practices do not need another SaaS dashboard. They need the prior auths worked, the lapsed patients called, the no-shows followed up, the callbacks answered at 9pm. That is what ZxAI AI staff do.

We provide nine AI staff roles: Front Desk, Scheduling, Prior Auth, Revenue Recovery, Reactivation, Retention, Reputation, Marketing, and Web / SEO. Practices hire the roles they need. The AI staff show up and do the job. Nobody on the practice's team has to manage a platform.

Who we serve

Independent practices in Texas.

Concierge medicine, functional medicine, and med spas. Revenue between $500K and $5M per year. One to ten providers. DFW, Austin, Houston, San Antonio.

We do not serve PE-owned groups, health systems, or practices under one year old. If you are focused on cutting costs rather than recovering revenue, we are not the right fit.

How we are different

Three architectural choices.
